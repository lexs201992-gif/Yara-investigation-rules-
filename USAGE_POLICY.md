# Usage Policy (Defensive-Only)

## Mission Constraint
All content in this repository is provided **exclusively for defensive cybersecurity purposes**:
- Containment
- Detection
- Incident response
- Recovery and resilience

## Explicitly Authorized Use Cases
1. Firmware and artifact scanning in authorized environments.
2. SOC/CSIRT triage and forensic validation.
3. MDM/EDR policy support for enterprise/government hardening.
4. Threat intelligence exchange for public safety.

## Prohibited Use Cases
You may **not** use this repository to:
- Develop, test, or improve offensive malware capabilities.
- Perform unauthorized intrusion, lateral movement, or persistence.
- Build evasion logic to bypass security controls.
- Conduct surveillance or targeting of civilians.

## Handling of Detections
A YARA match is an **indicator**, not final legal attribution.
Users must validate detections through:
- Multi-source corroboration
- Chain-of-custody evidence handling
- Peer review and reproducibility checks

## Responsible Disclosure and Coordination
When high-confidence findings may impact public safety, coordinate with appropriate authorities and affected vendors using responsible disclosure practices.

## Legal Compliance
Users are responsible for complying with all applicable local and international laws, regulations, and organizational policies.
